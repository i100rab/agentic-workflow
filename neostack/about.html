About NeoStack

We plan technology change the way engineers plan a bridge: measured twice, built once.

Our Approach

NeoStack doesn't do rip-and-replace unless rip-and-replace is actually the answer. Most transformation work is staged, reversible, and boring by design — because boring is what keeps a client's production systems up during a migration. We'd rather deliver four quiet cutovers than one dramatic one.

Where We Work

Founded in Sydney in 2016, with a Melbourne office opened in 2021. We work directly with mid-market and enterprise teams across Australia, and remotely with clients further afield when the engagement calls for it.

Leadership

Founder and Managing Director Elena Kovacs spent eleven years leading infrastructure and platform teams inside a major Australian bank before founding NeoStack in 2016. She still reviews every migration plan before a cutover date is set.

Most transformation failures aren't technical. They're sequencing failures — someone tried to do in one step what should have been four. — Elena Kovacs, Managing Director
