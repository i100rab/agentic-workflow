Services

Four pillars, sequenced around how your business actually runs.

Engagements are scoped from one pillar or all four, depending on where the constraint actually is.

01

Cloud & Modernisation

Migrate off legacy infrastructure and re-platform core systems onto modern, cost-efficient architecture — staged so production keeps running throughout.

  • Legacy-to-cloud migration (lift-and-shift through full re-platform)
  • Application modernisation and containerisation
  • Cost and workload optimisation for existing cloud spend
  • Infrastructure-as-code rollout and CI/CD pipeline setup
02

Cybersecurity & Risk

Reduce attack surface and bring systems into line with the compliance frameworks your industry and customers require.

  • Security posture assessment and penetration testing coordination
  • Zero-trust network architecture design
  • Compliance readiness (ISO 27001, Essential Eight, APRA CPS 234)
  • Incident response planning and tabletop exercises
03

Data & AI Enablement

Turn fragmented, siloed data into something the business can actually query, report on, and build on.

  • Data platform consolidation across legacy systems
  • Analytics and reporting layer implementation
  • AI / ML readiness assessment and pilot scoping
  • Data governance and quality frameworks
04

Managed IT & Support

Ongoing operations once the transformation lands, so the system stays healthy and someone's actually on call.

  • 24/7 monitoring, alerting, and incident response
  • Patch and vulnerability management
  • Helpdesk and end-user support
  • Quarterly infrastructure and cost reviews
